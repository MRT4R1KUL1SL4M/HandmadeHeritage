Inquiry Manifest কী তথ্য দেবেন

To ensure high-priority resolution, collectors must include a complete Issue Manifest in their correspondence:
  • Unique Order ID and checkout credentials (Email/Phone).
  • Precise description of the discrepancy and its chronological start.
  • High-resolution photographic evidence for any visual or physical defects.
  • Delivery coordinates for inquiries regarding logistics delays.

Response Protocol উত্তর প্রদানের সময়

Handmade Heritage prioritizes critical artisan issues according to a strict Response Service Level (SLA):
  • Standard Inquiry: Within 6 to 24 hours during business cycles.
  • Peak Operational Volume: Up to 48 hours during holiday surges.
  • Critical Integrity Claims: Reports of damage filed within 24 hours are elevated to priority status.
