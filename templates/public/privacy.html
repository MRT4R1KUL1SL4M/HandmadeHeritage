Privacy Governance গোপনীয়তা নীতি

Handmade Heritage operates under a Strict Privacy Mandate. We collect only the essential data required to facilitate artisan commerce and maintain platform integrity. Your personal data is never treated as a commodity; we do not sell or trade user information.

Data Acquisition কী তথ্য নেওয়া হয়

Our data collection is governed by necessity and transparency. We acquire:
  • Account Credentials: Name, email, and encrypted contact identifiers.
  • Acquisition Metadata: Delivery coordinates, item manifests, and logistics status.
  • Financial Signatures: Transaction references only (Full payment details are never stored).
  • Interaction Analytics: Technical device profiles to ensure interface stability.

Processing Protocols ব্যবহারের উদ্দেশ্য

Information processing is strictly limited to operational excellence:
  • Account Management and Identity Authentication.
  • Logistics Coordination with verified courier partners.
  • Security Audits to detect and neutralize fraudulent behavior.
  • Mandatory compliance with legal and jurisdictional requirements.

Data Sovereignty আপনার অধিকার

You maintain Absolute Rights over your digital footprint within our collective. You may exercise the following mandates:
  • Immediate Access to all stored personal signatures.
  • Mandatory Correction of inaccurate information.
  • Data Erasure where legal and financial obligations allow.

Security Architecture নিরাপত্তা কাঠামো

We utilize Reasonable Safeguards to protect artifact and collector data. This includes restricted access controls and encrypted storage layers.
USERS ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR PROTECTING THEIR OWN ACCESS KEYS AND OTP CODES. NEVER DISCLOSE CREDENTIALS TO THIRD PARTIES.
