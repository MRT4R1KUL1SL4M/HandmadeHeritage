The Legacy Story আমাদের গল্প

Handmade Heritage serves as the authoritative bridge between master artisans and global collectors. We exist to ensure that ancient craftsmanship thrives within the modern digital landscape through transparency and a premium commercial experience.

Rigorous Curation নির্বাচন প্রক্রিয়া

We maintain an uncompromising standard for our gallery. Every listing undergoes a strict verification protocol before being showcased:
  • Technique Verification: Confirmation of genuine handmade methods.
  • Material Integrity: Auditing of raw materials for heritage compliance.
  • Logistics Readiness: Mandatory museum-grade packaging standards.

Artisan Authenticity মান ও আসলত্ব

Minor character variations are the hallmark of true handmade art. However, structural defects are never tolerated.
If an artifact fails to reflect the promised artisan quality, we exercise full authority to rectify the acquisition through our mediation protocols.

Fair Trade Mandate ন্যায্য মূল্য

Handmade Heritage enforces Pricing Transparency. We demand that every listing reflects the true value of artisan labor, materials, and specialized techniques. Exploitative industrial pricing has no place in our collective.

Future Sustainability টেকসই উদ্যোগ

We protect the future of craft through responsible commercial practices.
  • Mandatory right-sized, responsible packaging.
  • Promotion of long-life materials to reduce environmental impact.
  • Bespoke care guidance to ensure artifacts endure for generations.
