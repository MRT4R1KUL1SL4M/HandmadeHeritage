Delivery Timelines আনুমানিক সময়

Handmade Heritage coordinates with elite global couriers to ensure the safe passage of your acquisition. Timelines are strictly estimates.
  • Local (Bangladesh): 2–4 business days post-dispatch.
  • International: 5–12 business days depending on destination and customs clearance.
  • High-Volume Alert: Holiday seasons require an additional 1–3 business days for logistics processing.

Artisan Processing প্রস্তুতির সময়

Quality cannot be rushed. Every masterpiece undergoes a mandatory inspection and preparation period.
  • Standard processing duration is 1–3 business days.
  • Custom or made-to-order items follow extended preparation schedules as noted on product manifests.
Heritage items often require specific finishing or drying times. We prioritize product integrity over rapid dispatch.

Shipment Monitoring ট্র্যাকিং

Customs & Duty Protocol কাস্টমস

International acquisitions cross sovereign borders and are subject to local governance.
  • Import duties and clearance fees are the sole legal liability of the buyer.
  • Handmade Heritage does not facilitate or oversee tax negotiations with local authorities.

Preservation Packaging প্যাকেজিং

We utilize museum-grade packaging protocols to ensure the preservation of artisan artifacts.
  • Fragile items feature multi-layer impact shielding.
  • Textiles are hermetically sealed against environmental moisture and contaminants.
